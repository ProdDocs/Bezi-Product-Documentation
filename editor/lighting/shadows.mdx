## Adding shadows to a scene

1. Toggle shadows on/off in the right sidebar

2. Once toggled on, the properties used to customize shadows will appear

<Info>
  Too many shadows in a VR scene can impact performance. Learn more about [optimizing your scene for VR](https://bezi.mintlify.app/editor/ar-vr/optimize-scenes-for-vr).&#x20;
</Info>

## Shadow properties

Shadows are derived from global Directional Light in a scene. A shadow's appearance can be customized using the below properties.&#x20;

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Light direction" defaultOpen={false}>
    Control the rotation of the global Directional Light.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Shadow intensity" defaultOpen={false}>
    Control the strength of the global Directional Light.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Shadow materials

Shadow materials can be applied to objects to make them completely transparent, except for shadows casted on them by other objects.&#x20;

Read more about [Shadow materials and their properties](https://bezi.mintlify.app/editor/materials/materials#shadow-material) here.&#x20;
