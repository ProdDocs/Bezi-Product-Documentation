## Adding shadows to a scene

1. Toggle shadows on/off in the right sidebar

2. Once toggled on, the properties used to customize shadows will appear

\[gif]

## Shadow properties

Shadows are derived from global Directional Light in a scene. A shadow's appearance can be customized using the below properties.&#x20;

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Light direction" defaultOpen={false}>
    Control the rotation of the global Directional Light.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Shadow intensity" defaultOpen={false}>
    Control the strength of the global Directional Light.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Shadow materials

Shadow materials can be applied to objects to make them completely transparent, except for shadows casted on them by other objects.

Read about Shadow materials and their properties here \[link to Materials page > Shadow materials section]
