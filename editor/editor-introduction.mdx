## **Welcome to the Bezi Editor Documentation Hub!**&#x20;

Bezi Editor is a web-based, collaborative 3D canvas designed for creating and prototyping 3D games and experiences. Here, you’ll find comprehensive documentation covering all of Bezi’s features, along with [**Bezi 101 tutorial videos**](https://www.bezi.com/bezi-101) to help you get started.&#x20;

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to email us at [**support@bezi.com**](mailto:support@bezi.com). You can also join the conversation on our [**Community page**](https://community.bezi.com/home), where you can ask questions, share ideas, and get responses from our team.

We can’t wait to see what you create—let’s turn your ideas into reality!
