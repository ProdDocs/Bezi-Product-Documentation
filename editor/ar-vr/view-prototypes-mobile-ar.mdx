<Accordion title="Anchoring scenes to the real world in mobile AR " defaultOpen={false}>
  1. Follow the below steps to open the prototype in Play Mode

  2. A prompt to "set an anchor" will appear on the mobile device. As the device is moved, Bezi will detect a horizontal surface and place the scene origin on it

  3. Tap on the device screen to set the anchor

  4. To reposition the anchor, select the **Reposition** button and repeat these steps
</Accordion>

## Open AR scene on iOS device

1. Open the Editor file on a desktop

2. Click the **Play** dropdown in the top toolbar to view device options

3. Select **Mobile** from the device options

4. A popover with a QR code and instructions will appear

5. Install the [XR Browser app](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/xr-browser/id1588029989) on the iOS device&#x20;

6. Open and enable camera permissions for the XR Browser app&#x20;

7. Tap the address bar in the XR Browser app&#x20;

8. Select the **QR code** icon&#x20;

9. Scan the QR code in the desktop popover

10. Click the **AR** button in the top right to initiate AR view mode

11. Move the iOS device to enjoy the prototype in AR

## Open AR scene on Android device

1. Open the Editor file on a desktop

2. Click the **Play** **dropdown** in the top toolbar to view device options

3. Select **Mobile** from the device options

4. Open the camera app on the Android device

5. Scan the QR code in the desktop popover

6. Click the **AR** button in the top right to initiate AR view mode

7. Move the Android device to enjoy the prototype in AR
