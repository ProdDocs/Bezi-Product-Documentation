## Publishing a prototype as a public link

When a prototype is published as a public link, **anyone with the link** can view and interact with it in Play Mode. Viewers will only see the prototype and will not be edit it, comment on it, or see any of the Editor UI.&#x20;

Enable open sharing and viewing of a prototype in Play Mode.

## Publishing a prototype public link \[missing asset]

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Select the **Share** button in the top right of the toolbar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2">
    Click the **Publish link** section in the right side of the Share modal
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 3">
    In the Publish link view, toggle on **Publish to web**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 4">
    Select **Copy link** to copy the public link to your clipboard

    \[asset]
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Disabling a prototype public link&#x20;

Disabling an active prototype public link will make the public link inactive for all external viewers. Anyone that attempts to open the public link will see "File not found" once it's disabled.

To disable a prototype's public link, open the Publish link section in the Share modal (follow steps 1 and 2 above) and toggle off **Publish to the web**.
