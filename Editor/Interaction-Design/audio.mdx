## Using Audio objects \[missing assets & numbering]

There are a two ways you can add Audio to a scene. Once added, Audio objects can be identified in a scene by the purple speaker icon.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Adding Audio from the Objects menu" defaultOpen={false}>
    An Audio track will automatically be assigned to your Audio object when adding from the Objects menu. [The default Audio track can be easily changed](/7784c4710e7440b0ba578621f782d553?pvs=25#db178f46953f43fbac2e42cef9095849).

    Open the **Objects** menu in the top toolbar

    Select Audio from the dropdown

    Once select, click in your scene to place the Audio object
