## Adding shadows to a scene

1. Toggle shadows on/off in the right sidebar

2. Once toggled on, the properties used to customize shadows will appear

\[asset]

## Shadow properties

Shadows are caused by global directional light in a scene. These properties can be used to customize a shadow's appearance.&#x20;
