## How does Bezi manage and protect my credit card and payment information?

We use Stripe to securely manage subscriptions and billing, so all changes will be made in your personal billing portal.

## How do I update my subscription or billing information?

Access your billing portal to set-up a subscription after your trial expires, update credit card information, view and download invoices, or cancel a subscription.

**How to access your billing portal:**

1. Navigate to **Your Plan** within Bezi's Account Settings
2. Select `Upgrade Plan` (if on a free trial) or `Manage Subscription` (if on the Pro plan)
3. A secure billing portal webpage will open in your default browser. From here you can update your subscription and billing information

## Where can I get my invoices or receipts?

Your entire billing history, along with invoices and receipts, is available in your billing portal.

1. Follow the above steps to access your billing portal
2. Navigate to the **Invoice History** section
3. Select the month you want to download an invoice or receipt for \> select what to download
