## How credit usage is measured

This depends on the model used, task complexity and size - specifically, how many steps Bezi takes to complete the work.

1. Open [accout dashboard](https://app.bezi.com/account) \> **Usage** tab
2. Toggle on **Enable usage-based pricing**
3. Set your monthly spending limit
4. Click `Save`
5. Log out of Bezi completely and back-in before continuing your work

## Viewing your usage

To see a per-prompt breakdown of credit usage, including which model was used and how many credits each prompt consumed:

1. Open [app.bezi.com/account](https://app.bezi.com/account)
2. Select the **Subscription & Usage** tab

## On-demand usage

If you hit your plan's credit limit before renewal, you can enable on-demand credits to keep working.

1. Open [app.bezi.com/account](https://app.bezi.com/account)
1. Select the **Subscriptions & Usage** tab
2. Toggle on **Enable on-demand usage**
3. Set your monthly spending limit
4. Click `Save`

Overage credits are priced at **\$0.05 per credit**. Charges are applied at regular intervals, not at the end of the month. If a payment fails, you won't be able to prompt Bezi until the payment succeeds.

## Credit reset at billing renewal

Credits reset to your plan's monthly limit at each billing renewal. Unused credits from the previous cycle do not roll over.

See [Billing](https://docs.bezi.com/account/billing) for information on upgrading, downgrading, and managing your subscription.
