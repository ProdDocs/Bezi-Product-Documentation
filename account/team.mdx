title: "Teams"
---

<Note>
  Teams are only available on Team and Enterprise plans.
</Note>

A Team is an organization-level hub, created for groups with Team or Enterprise plans. A Team contains the following:

- **Team Roles (Admin or Member)**; Admin(s) can view, add, manage and monitor all Member licenses and usage in one place
- [**Team Workspaces**](https://docs.bezi.com/fundamentals/team-workspaces); A Team can hold endless Team Workspaces. Each Team Workspace is unique, with it's own subset of members and shared materials for a given purpose. Read more about a Team Workspace here
- **Credit pool**; A Team's monthly credits are shared across all Team Members so everyone has access to the credits they need as Unity work fluctuates. Adding extra team members will add more credits to the total pool

## Team Roles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Team Admin">
    - Can invite and remove Team members
    - Can purchase and manage seats
    - Can manage Team-level settings
    - Can create Team Workspaces
    - Can invite existing Team members to Workspaces
    - Create and edit content within Team Workspaces they belong to 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Team Member">
    - Can create Team Workspaces
    - Can invite existing Team members to Workspaces
    - Create and edit content within Team Workspaces they belong to
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Creating a Team and adding members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up a Team plan">
    To create a Team, follow these steps:

    1. **For new users**: Visit [bezi.com/pricing](https://www.bezi.com/pricing) and select the Team plan 
    2. **For existing users**: Go to your [account dashboard](https://app.bezi.com/account) and click `Upgrade to Team`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your Team Org in the Bezi app">
    1. Open **Account settings**
    2. Navigate to the **Subscription** section
    3. Confirm the account labeled`Team`  is selected, or select it from the  dropdown 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up the Team and invite members">
    1. Navigate to the **Subscription** section of **Account settings**
    2. Click `Team Settings `
    3. Go to **Team members** section
    4. Enter email addresses and send the invites

    If all purchased seats are full,  invites will be blocked. The Team Admin must purchase more seats or free up existing ones before more invites can be sent. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage and remove Team members">
    1. Navigate to **Account settings** \> **Subscription** section
    2. Click `Team Settings `
    3. Go to **Team members** section
    4. Remove the desired Team members 

    When someone is removed from the Team, their access to all Workspaces is fully revoked. Any Shared Pages they created or added to will remain untouched. 
  </Step>
</Steps>
