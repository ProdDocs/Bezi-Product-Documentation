title: "Usage Analytics and Limits"
---

View Bezi usage metrics, track adoption patterns, and set credit limits in the Usage dashboard.

<Note>
  Per-Member usage data and limits are only available on Enterprise plans.
</Note>

## What’s team analytics and limits?

Enterprise account Admins can now view usage data and set usage limits per team member.

Members can still view their credit usage in the [Accounts](https://app.bezi.com/account) dashboard.

## Admin Access and Permissions

### What Admins can see

- **Usage overview:** the account org’s total credit pool and credit usage by member
- **Spend Limits**: a table of each Member’s **Custom Spend Limit**, **Spend to Date**, and **Pending Requests**
- **Usage History:** complete **Usage History** log for every Member. Records of the user, date, mode, model type, and credits consumed, filterable by time range, mode, and model type.
