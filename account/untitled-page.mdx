title: "Usage Analytics and Limits"
---

View Bezi usage metrics, track adoption patterns, and set credit limits in the Usage dashboard.

<Note>
  Per-Member usage data and limits are only available on Enterprise plans.
</Note>

## What’s team analytics and limits?

Admins of Enterprise accounts can now view usage data for each team member and set usage limits per member.

All members can still view their credit usage in [Accounts](https://app.bezi.com/account).
